About Us

Introduction.

Nigeria remains a fertile ground for investments for discerning investors, particularly, Real Estate which has shown a great resilience despite the recent economic strong winds and as a result has emerged as the third largest contribution to our GDP.

It will be naive to discountenance the labyrinth of statutory and social impediments associated with investing in real estate in Nigeria. This is where , Savills Properties having identified the gaps, would deploy its expertise to ensure successful delivery of property projects and ancillary investments, to Nigerians in the diaspora.

Mission Statement

To provide secure, transparent, and innovative real estate solutions that empower Nigerians in the diaspora to confidently invest in and own properties in Nigeria, ensuring their hard-earned income is safeguarded while delivering high-quality projects that meet their aspirations.

Vision Statement

To be the leading real estate company trusted by Nigerians in the diaspora for exceptional service, transparency, and professionalism, driving transformative growth in Nigeria's real estate sector while creating enduring value for our clients and communities.

Our Approach

At Savills Properties, we have assembled a seasoned team of professionals, including real estate lawyers, quantity surveyors, project managers, and engineers, to assist prospective property owners in achieving their goals without unnecessary risk or loss. To ensure transparency and security, we have partnered with a reputable firm of trustees, a subsidiary of a financial institution with a balance sheet exceeding ₦100 billion. The trustees act as custodians of development funds, holding and disbursing them only in accordance with the client's explicit instructions.
